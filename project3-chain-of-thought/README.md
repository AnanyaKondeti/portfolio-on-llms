# 🧠 Project 3: Chain of Thought Prompting

## What it does
Compares normal prompting vs Chain of Thought (CoT) prompting on math problems.

## Key Finding
- For simple math, both approaches give similar results
- CoT shows its real power on complex multi-step reasoning
- CoT makes AI reasoning transparent and easier to debug

## Why it matters
CoT is used in production LLM systems to improve accuracy on 
reasoning tasks by forcing the model to show its work.

## Tech Used
- Python, Groq API, LLaMA 3.1
